WELCOME TO JAVASCRIPT BATTLESHIP!

The game is simple, drag and drop your ships onto the player board into the positions you want, after that you can start the game. Once started you must try eliminate the enemy ships before they eliminate yours! Click where on the computer board you would like to target, a grey square indicates a miss, red indicates a hit! Be careful not to group up your ships, or the enemy might find you too easily! The game ends when the first player looses all ships, good luck!
